We are an agriculture company engaged in Manufacturing and delivering reliable and best quality gentically pure seed, Organic Soil nutrients and Organic crop Protection. Srk Agritech india Private Limited belive that the growing food grain, oil-seeds and vegetable demand of the country can only be fullfilled by providing genetically pure, best quality Agriculture seeds to farmers, which there by increase the productivity and profitability of the farmers or growers.

SRK AGRITECH, recognising the importance of quality seed and Productivity of farmer, modernising its activities and processes since 2003. Adoption of seed quality managment technique, fundamental changes in the farming philosophy and providing soil nutrients for enhanching and optimising soil health through organic Manure.

Our Efficient work force works together right from the pre sowing till the harvesting of the seed, our storage and processing facilities helping us to maintain seed quality after post harvest of the seed.

NCMSL (National Collateral Managment Service Limited) Provide us with Scientific Storage of our seed where again quality is maintained.

We have highly qualified processor with an experience of more than 10 years in seed processing which adds an advantage for maintaining quality at it's best.

SRK Agritech Organic Fertilizer

Environmental protection being integral to Srk Agritech's Business strategies, the company is committed to bringing down Chemical fertilizer use and progressively reducing any negative impact of its operations through the adoption of sustainable practices. Our Organic Fertilizer performance may be categorised in the following ways:

 

  • Effective Soil Health management systems are in place to ensure soil moisture, Soil Conditioning and soil optimizing.
  • Strong Plant, Increased Resistant To root Borer and Increased Per acre Yeild.
  • Continuous research and development of new organic Fertilizers to improve the per acre yeild and soil.
  • Provides excellent overall effect on plant growth, Induces developmen of new branches / leaves that improves the quality of production.
  • Improves and form Soil structure, texture, improves aeration and water holding capacity and prevents Soil erosion.
  • Increases in soil organic matter decomposition. Enriched with important vitamins, enzymes and hormones such as auxins, gibberellins.
