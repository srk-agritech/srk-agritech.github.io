We work closely with our growers, Suggesting them best suited products for there crops and thereby helping them to achieve the aim of “Produce more from less.” Also helping our growers to maximize the value of every acre they cultivate. We work with them and build relationships by listening to our farmers or growers, solving there problems with best suited solutions, we visit their field by walking and sharing technical resources with them. We are building distribution chain so as to make our product available at right time, at right price, at right quantity, at right place.

Wherever our grower or farmers purchase SRK AGRITECH BRAND seed, they get access to pool of information regarding sowing techniques, product and amount of product to be sown, our leading services including agronomy (related to soil health, soil conditioner, and soil moisture holding capacity), online services and information, risk management solution.
