Variety Name & Features - PURVA


  • Irrigated Timely Sown. Duration 110-118 Days.

  • Plant Height 91-95 CM.

  • The Grain Size Is Bold And Lustrous Amber Colour.

  • Possesses High Degree Of Resistance To Both Leaf And Stem Rusts.

Variety Name & Features - Ajeyta


  • Irrigated Timely Sown. Duration 98-110 Days.

  • Plant Height 85-90 CM.

  • Best For Chapaties, Higher Market Rates, Grain Medium Bold And Lustrous.

  • Non-Lodging, Non-Shattering, Suitable For Low To High Fertility

  • Average Yeild 50-55 Qtl/HA.

Variety Name & Features - Param


  • Irrigated Late Sown And Very Late Sown. Duration 95-115 Days.

  • Plant Height 69-85 CM.

  • It has very good chapatti and excellent bread making qualities.

  • Tolerance To Heat Stress, Resistance to brown rust and black rust.

  • Average Yeild 40-48 Qtl/HA.
