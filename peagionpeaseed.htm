Pigeonpea seed (Also Known as ARHAR & TUR) consist lots of nutritional value, pigeonpea is an important cereal diet included in indian food habits, which is highly enriched in Protien (20%-22%) fibers and minerals. Above 90% of the global peagion pea is grown in india. Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Maharastra Madhypradesh, Uttar Pradesh,Bihar, Karnatka etc contributes 82.5% of total production Basket.

Each and every part of peagion pea is of great use. The stalks of pigeonpea are used as fuel. The Pigeonpea is mainly used in dal form in india wihich contains high nutritional value

At Various agro climatic regions, different maturity group of pigeonpea are grown they are as Follows:-

Long Duration- This maturity group takes 250-280 days. Grown in North Eastern Plains Zone.

Medium Duration- This maturity group takes 160-180 days. Grown in Central India and South India.

Short Duration- This maturity group takes 130-150 days. Grown in North Weatern Region under different

cropping system.

Should be sown in 2-3 cm 3-5 Cm Plant to plant

Pigeonpea Fertilizer Management (PPFM).

    PPFM for cost benefit

    It is advised to spread Srk Agritech Orman brand organic fertilizer. Because Orman increases soil fertility, soil texture and increases soil moisture holding capacity. If a farmer use Orman 100 kg per hectare than it is adviced to reduce the NPK content to half that is 15:40:20 and 20 kg sulphur per hectare. This provide cost benefit to the farmer and increases the organic matter in the soil which will boodt the plant growth and hece increses the yeild per acre.

    SFM with Non-Organic

  • It is advised to follow NPKS (Nitrogen, phosphorus,potash, sulphur)at 30:80:40:40 kg/per hectare for best yeild.
  • The huge demand for Fertilizers in plant begins at pod filling stage to ten days til seed forms.

    • Water Management In Pigeonpea

  • Though Pigeonpea crop doesn't requires any irrigation during the kharif season because of good monsoon. But in case if there is low rainfall than irrigation should be given at flowering and pod filling stage. With this there will be good yeild for the farmers who has irrigation facility.
