Soybean seed consist lots of nutritional value, soybean contains 40 % (Forty Percent) of protein around 19% - 22% oil. Soybean is being used for making many health foods due to its high nutritional value. Soybean mainly processed for making edible oil. It is also processed for making soymilk, soy-paneer (tofu), nuggets, flour etc.

In India Madhya Pradesh and Maharashtra counts 85% of total soybean production. Some parts of Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, himachal Pradesh, Punjab and Delhi.

Soybean requires 26.5 ° C -30°C optimum temperatures for most varieties.

SRK Agritech Soybean Cultivation Tips

Before sowing treat the seed with srk agritech seed treatmeant provided in the seed bag which will give effective germination and groth for the plant.

Soybean Fertilizer Management (SFM).

    SFM for cost benefit

    It is advised to spread Srk Agritech Orman brand organic fertilizer. Because Orman increases soil fertility, soil texture and increases soil moisture holding capacity. If a farmer use Orman 100 kg per hectare than it is adviced to reduce the NPK content to half that is 15:40:20 and 20 kg sulphur per hectare. This provide cost benefit to the farmer and increases the organic matter in the soil which will boodt the plant growth and hece increses the yeild per acre.

    SFM with Non-Organic

  • It is advised to follow NPKS (Nitrogen, phosphorus,potash, sulphur)at 30:80:40:40 kg/per hectare for best yeild.
  • The huge demand for Fertilizers in plant begins at pod filling stage to ten days til seed forms.

    • Water Management In Soybean

  • Though Soybean crop doesn't requires any irrigation during the kharif season because of good monsoon. But in case if there is low rainfall than irrigation should be given at flowering and pod filling stage. With this there will be good yeild for the farmers who has irrigation facility.
